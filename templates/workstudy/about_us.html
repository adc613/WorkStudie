What is WorkStudie?

WorkStudie is a platform similar to cragslist where people are allow to post task for cash rewards, except that WorkStudie is designed in paticular for college students who live hetic and at times lazy lives. If you ever have a task you don't want to do post it and another trusted memember of you're own student body will complete it for you.

Studiers, create the task.

A studier is someone who has a task that needs doing, but doesn't have the desire or motivation to do it. Examples would be cleaning, help tutoring, grocery shopping, laundry, help moving, or any task that they can think of. They set a price they are willing to pay and then workers bid to complete the task and the studier will choose their worker.

Workers, get paid to complete the tasks.

Workers are college students who are a little low on cash or just looking to make some more cash. Once They bid to complete a task they will complete the task in a timely manor.
